About Tanah Lot

The Great Temple of Tanah Lot

Perched atop a rugged offshore rock formation, this ancient Hindu shrine captivates visitors with its dramatic silhouette against the sunset-dappled horizon..

History and Beauty of Tanah Lot

Pura Luhur Tanah Lot is located on a coral island located a bit to the edge, in Segara Kidul Tanah Lot. The location of the coral island that is the location of Pura Luhur Tanah Lot like that gave birth to several stories about the origin of the name of the coral island which are put forward in a pantheon or mythology. The area of Tanah Lot itself is 28.58 Ha which includes the Temple, Housing, Tourist Lodging, Office, Park, Wantilan, Parking, Rice Fields and Roads.
